## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The information we collect depends on how you use our products.

### A. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Food and Meal Data:** We collect the text, audio, and image data you input to analyze and track your calorie intake.
*   **Audio Data:** When you use the voice input feature, we access your device's microphone to record your voice and transcribe it into text. This data is used solely for the purpose of logging your meals.
*   **Image Data:** When you select images from your photo library for analysis, we process these images to identify food items and estimate nutritional information.

### B. Information We Collect Automatically
*   **Device Information:** We may collect information about the device you use to access our Service, such as the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (like Advertising ID).
*   **Usage Data:** We collect information about your activity on our application, such as the features you use and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
*   **Ad Data:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information about your device and your interaction with ads to provide personalized advertising.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:
*   Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
*   Improve, personalize, and expand our Service.
*   Analyze how you use our Service to improve user experience.
*   Process your voice and image inputs to provide calorie and nutritional analysis.
*   Show you advertisements that are relevant to your interests (via Google AdMob).

## 3. Permissions

To provide our Service, we require certain permissions on your mobile device:

*   **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO):** Required to capture your voice for meal logging.
*   **Speech Recognition:** Required to transcribe your voice input into text.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but some features of the Service may no longer function properly.
